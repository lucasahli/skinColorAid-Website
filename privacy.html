Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

1. Overview

Respecting your privacy is not just a legal requirement for us; it is a core principle. SkinColorAid does not collect, store, share, or sell your personal data.

2. Data Collection

The App operates entirely locally on your macOS device.

  • We do not transmit screen contents to any server.
  • We do not analyze your images for facial recognition or identity.
  • All color analysis is performed in Random Access Memory (RAM) and is discarded immediately after the overlay is drawn.

3. Screen Recording Permissions

To function, SkinColorAid requires "Screen Recording" permission on macOS. This is strictly used to sample the pixel colors under your mouse cursor or within the selection area to provide color feedback. This data never leaves your computer.
